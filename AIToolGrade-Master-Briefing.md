## Site Positioning
Research-based, NOT a hands-on testing site. Every review is built from primary source documentation, verified pricing, community sentiment, and structured scoring. Never claim personal testing.

Prohibited language: "we tested", "we used", "30-day trial", "game changer", "must-try", "no-brainer", "genuinely impressive", "genuinely transformative", "changes everything", "revolutionary"
