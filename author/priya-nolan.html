Priya is a former content strategist who pivoted fully into the AI space after watching artificial intelligence quietly transform every tool in her stack. She writes about AI from the perspective of the everyday professional — the freelancers, marketers, small business owners, and creatives navigating a rapidly changing landscape without a technical background.

Her work focuses on making sense of where AI is going, how it's reshaping creative and knowledge work, and what it all means for the people actually doing the work.

Writing approach

Priya writes for the curious non-technical professional who wants to understand AI without feeling overwhelmed. She makes the unfamiliar feel accessible — and reminds readers that everyone is figuring this out together.
