What is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is an AI-powered writing assistant designed for marketers, entrepreneurs, and content creators who need fast, quality copy without a steep learning curve. It covers everything from blog posts and social media captions to email sequences and product descriptions.

We tested Copy.ai for 30 days across a range of real content tasks — short-form ads, long-form blog drafts, and email campaigns — to give you an honest picture of where it excels and where it falls short.

Pricing

Copy.ai offers a free plan that includes unlimited projects and access to core templates — one of the most generous free tiers in the AI writing space. The Pro plan runs $36/month and unlocks workflow automation, priority support, and advanced brand voice tools.

For teams, the Teams plan starts at $186/month for 5 seats. It's a significant jump, but the workflow automation alone can save dozens of hours per month for active content operations.

Who Should Use Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is the ideal starting point for anyone new to AI writing tools. The free plan is genuinely useful — not a crippled trial — and the interface makes it easy to get results in minutes without reading a manual.

If you're running a content-heavy business and need long-form quality at scale, you may eventually outgrow it and want to look at Jasper. But for most individual creators and small teams, Copy.ai hits a great balance of capability and cost.
