What is Leonardo AI?

Leonardo AI is a web-based AI image generation platform built around a token economy and a large library of fine-tuned models. Unlike Midjourney — which has no free plan and runs through a web interface alone — Leonardo has a genuinely useful free tier (150 tokens daily), a no-Discord-required web app, and tools for custom model training that let you build consistent visual styles. It's become the go-to starting point for creators who want more control than Midjourney offers without paying more for it.

Pricing

Leonardo's free plan gives 150 tokens per day — enough for 25-37 standard images, though advanced features eat through tokens faster. The Apprentice plan at $12/month (or $10/month annual) provides 8,500 monthly tokens and unlocks private image generation and commercial rights. Artisan at $24/month ($20 annual) jumps to 25,000 tokens with custom LoRA model training and API access. Maestro at $48/month ($40 annual) gives 60,000 tokens with priority queue and video generation.

One genuinely good feature: token rollover. Unused tokens carry over to a rollover bank on paid plans rather than disappearing at month end. For irregular users, that makes a lower plan more valuable in practice than the monthly numbers suggest.

Leonardo vs Midjourney

These two tools appeal to different types of creators. Midjourney wins on pure aesthetic quality — its images have a polish and intentionality that Leonardo's output doesn't consistently match. If you're generating editorial art, concept work, or anything where you want images that feel artistically considered, Midjourney is still ahead.

Leonardo wins everywhere else. It has a real free tier. It works in a browser without Discord. It offers more granular control over outputs through ControlNet and model selection. Custom LoRA training lets you build and reuse consistent styles — something Midjourney doesn't offer at comparable price points. For game developers, product designers, and social media creators who need volume and control, Leonardo often makes more sense.

Who should use Leonardo AI?

Leonardo is the right starting point for anyone new to AI image generation — the free tier is genuinely useful and there's no credit card required. It's also the right choice for creators who need custom model training, consistent character styles, or API integration at a reasonable price. If pure aesthetic quality is your top priority and you don't mind paying $10/month minimum, start with Midjourney instead.
